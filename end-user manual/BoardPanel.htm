The Board Panel

This panel shows the chess board

To make a move, click the piece with the mouse and drag it to the destination square, or simply click the piece and then click on the destination square.

You can always drop the piece back to it's original square. Dropping the piece in an illegal move square will also bring it back.
Board Popup

Right click inside the board panel to show the context menu:

Flip Board
Flip or turn the board around so that white pieces are on top.
Switch to Small Mode
Switch to the small component mode. See SmallModeWindow.
Zoom
Zoom or resize the board.
